Body Repair Service

Description:

Body repair service is necessary for restoring the appearance and structural integrity of a vehicle damaged in accidents or due to wear and tear. Skilled technicians use various techniques and equipment to repair dents, scratches, and structural damage to the vehicle's body.

Jobs Included:

  • Dent repair: Removing dents using techniques such as paintless dent repair (PDR) or traditional dent removal methods.
  • Scratch removal: Buffing, sanding, and polishing to remove surface scratches and restore the paint finish.
  • Panel replacement: Replacing damaged body panels with new ones to restore the vehicle's structural integrity and appearance.
  • Frame straightening: Using specialized equipment to realign the vehicle's frame or chassis if it has been bent or misaligned in an accident.
  • Paint touch-up: Matching and applying paint to repair areas where the paint has been chipped or scratched.
  • Rust repair: Removing rust and repairing affected areas to prevent further corrosion and structural damage.
