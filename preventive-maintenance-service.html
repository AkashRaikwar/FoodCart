Preventive Maintenance Service

Description:

Preventive maintenance service is essential for ensuring the long-term health and performance of your vehicle. It involves scheduled inspections, adjustments, and replacements of key components to prevent breakdowns and extend the lifespan of your car.

Jobs Included:

  • Engine oil change: Regularly changing the engine oil helps maintain engine lubrication and prevents premature wear and
  • Air filter replacement: The air filter prevents contaminants from entering the engine, and replacing it ensures optimal engine performance.
  • Fluid checks and top-ups: Inspecting and replenishing fluids such as coolant, brake fluid, power steering fluid, and transmission fluid to maintain proper levels and prevent issues.
  • Tire rotation and pressure check: Rotating tires promotes even wear and extends tire life, while maintaining correct tire pressure ensures safe handling and fuel efficiency.
  • Brake inspection: Checking brake pads, rotors, and brake fluid for wear and proper function to ensure reliable braking performance.
  • Battery check: Inspecting the battery for corrosion, leaks, and proper voltage to prevent starting issues.
  • Inspection of belts and hoses: Checking for signs of wear or damage and replacing them as needed to prevent unexpected failures.
